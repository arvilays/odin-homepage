THE STRAW HAT PIRATES
To the people of the Grand Line, East Blue, West Blue, South Blue, North Blue, and all territories under the World Government's jurisdiction:

It has come to our attention that the notorious Straw Hat Pirates, led by the fugitive Monkey D. Luffy, remain at large after their disappearance following the catastrophic incident at Sabaody Archipelago two years ago. Despite their mysterious absence from public view, we have reason to believe they are actively regrouping and preparing to resume their unlawful activities.

The Straw Hat Pirates have committed heinous acts of piracy, theft, and rebellion against the World Government. Their past actions have left devastation in their wake, and their return poses a significant threat to the peace and stability of our world.

The Marine Forces request the vigilance of every citizen. If you have any information regarding the whereabouts or activities of the Straw Hat Pirates, you are urged to report it immediately to your local Marine Base. Any person found aiding or abetting the Straw Hat Pirates will face swift and severe retribution under the law.

The World Government WILL NOT allow their return.

The Straw Hat Pirates are considered dangerous criminals with a bounty of unprecedented scale. They have evaded justice for far too long, and we are committed to bringing them to justice at all costs. Please remain alert and report any suspicious activity.

Together, we can ensure that these outlaws are brought to account for their crimes.

For peace. For justice.

- The Marines
